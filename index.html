Ever wonder who do
you recite like?

This AI powered web app might help you. Trained on recitation data of Sheikh Abdul Basit Abdul Samad, Mishary bin Rashid, Abu Bakr al-Shatri, Abdur-Rahman as-Sudais, Junaid Jamshed, and Omer Amin. It detects the similarities between your and the reciter's voice using CNNs. This App by no means accurate and is at a very early prototype stage.
